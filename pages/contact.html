Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive digital solutions including WordPress development, Shopify e-commerce stores, digital marketing campaigns, SEO optimization, graphic design, and content creation. I specialize in creating custom solutions tailored to your business needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple WordPress website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while complex e-commerce stores may take 3-4 weeks. I always provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

What are your pricing rates?

My rates vary based on project complexity and requirements. WordPress sites start from $500, Shopify stores from $800, and digital marketing services from $300/month. I provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I provide ongoing support and maintenance for all projects. This includes security updates, content updates, technical support, and performance optimization. Support packages are available based on your needs.

Can you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am experienced in managing projects across different time zones. I use modern communication tools and project management systems to ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept various payment methods including bank transfers, PayPal, Wise (formerly TransferWise), and cryptocurrency. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, with milestone payments for larger projects.
